A Passion for Timeless Beauty

At Deco by Mariem, we believe that design is a dialogue between the past and the present. Our philosophy is rooted in the belief that luxury is not about excess, but about the thoughtful calibration of space, light, and materiality.

Every stroke of our pencil and every material we source is driven by a desire to reach the sublime. We don't follow trends; we create environments that develop their own character over decades, becoming more beautiful as they are lived in.

We source our materials from local master artisans whose families have been perfecting their craft for generations. This dedication to raw authenticity, combined with modern architectural precision, allows us to deliver projects that are truly one of a kind.

From the intricate geometry of a hand-cut mosaic to the soft drape of Moroccan silk, our attention to detail is what defines us. We invite you to explore a world where every corner tells a story and every room evokes a memory.
