About Us

We Are a Passionate Luxury Interior Design Studio

Deco by Mariem was founded with a singular vision: to redefine luxury living through a lens of authenticity and elegance. Our founder, Mariem, envisioned a studio where the vibrant heritage of Morocco could meet the clean, sophisticated lines of modern global design.

Every project we undertake is a masterclass in bespoke craftsmanship. We don't just decorate rooms; we curate environments that reflect the unique personality of our clients, ensuring that every texture chosen and every light fixture placed contributes to a cohesive, soul-stirring story.

Timeless Design, Honest Vision, Pure Elegance

"Design is not just what it looks like and feels like. Design is how it works and the emotion it evokes."
