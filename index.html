We Are a Luxury Interior Design Studio

Founded on the principles of refined craftsmanship and enduring elegance, Deco by Mariem specializes in creating bespoke interiors that harmonize traditional Moroccan influences with modern luxury.

Our studio believes that every space has a story to tell. We curate materials, textures, and lighting to evoke emotions, ensuring that each project is as unique as the client who inhabits it.

From private villas to sophisticated penthouses, our team brings a meticulous attention to detail and a passion for perfection to every creative endeavor.
